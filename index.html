About Us

We are a specialized software development team focusing on automation solutions for Pakistan Customs export tasks. We've developed innovative software that significantly streamlines the export documentation process on Weboc.pk.com and Pakistan Single Window Portal, saving exporters valuable time and reducing errors in documentation.

Since 2020, our Web-R software has been helping exporters and export agencies across Pakistan automate their customs documentation processes.

Core Solution

Our flagship automation software Web-R addresses a critical pain point for Pakistani exporters who previously had to manually complete numerous entries on Weboc.pk.com. This tedious process was prone to errors and extremely time-consuming. Our solution automates these tasks, dramatically improving efficiency and accuracy.
